# Quick Start Guide

## Your First Week Checklist

This guide will help you hit the ground running with Aunt Liz's house renovation project.

* * *

## Day 1-2: Initial Assessment

### \[ \] Visit the House

- Take photos of every room (before pictures)
- Measure the 3 rooms that need carpet
- Note any additional repairs needed
- Assess furniture - keep, donate, or junk?

### \[ \] Create Photo Documentation

```
photos/
  before/
    living-room-1.jpg
    kitchen-1.jpg
    bedroom-1.jpg
    etc.
```

* * *

## Day 3-4: Get Quotes

### \[ \] Contact Contractors (Minimum 3)

**What to ask:**

- "I need a quote for painting the entire interior, including cabinets"
- "We also need carpet removed and new carpet installed in 3 bedrooms"
- "Plus a back door and window sill replacement"
- Ask about their availability and timeline
- Request references

Document quotes in: `VENDORS.md`

### \[ \] Visit Paint Stores

- [ ] Go to Sherwin-Williams or Benjamin Moore
- [ ] Get samples of recommended colors:
    - [ ] Agreeable Gray (SW 7029)
    - [ ] Edgecomb Gray (BM HC-173)
    - [ ] White Dove (BM OC-17)
- [ ] Cost: ~\$5 per sample

### \[ \] Visit Carpet Stores

- [ ] Home Depot (free in-home measure)
- [ ] Lowe's (free in-home measure)
- [ ] Local carpet store
- [ ] Ask about: Mohawk SmartStrand or Shaw Caress
- [ ] Get samples in beige/greige colors

**Document in:** `MATERIALS.md`

* * *

## Day 5-6: Make Decisions

### \[ \] Test Paint Colors

- Paint large swatches (2ft x 2ft) on walls
- Check in different lighting throughout the day
- Get family/friends opinions
- Take photos to compare

### \[ \] Select Carpet

- Compare quotes
- Choose color (neutral beige or greige)
- Verify installation timeline
- Check lead time (often 2-4 weeks!)

### \[ \] Review Budget

- Compare contractor quotes
- Calculate total project cost
- Ensure financing is in place
- Approve budget

**Update:** `BUDGET.md`

* * *

## Day 7: Lock Everything In

### \[ \] Make Final Decisions

- [ ] Select winning contractor
- [ ] Finalize paint colors
- [ ] Finalize carpet choice
- [ ] Set start date

### \[ \] Place Orders

- [ ] Order paint (if doing yourself)
- [ ] Order carpet (long lead time!)
- [ ] Order back door
- [ ] Sign contractor agreements

### \[ \] Update Timeline

- [ ] Confirm all dates with contractor
- [ ] Schedule donation pickup
- [ ] Mark calendar for key milestones

Update: `TIMELINE.md`

## Money-Saving Tips

### DIY to Save \$\$

- Remove curtains yourself (save \$0 - already free)
- Remove carpet yourself (save \$200-400)
- Paint yourself (save \$2,000-3,000)
- Clean yourself (save \$300-600)

### Smart Shopping

- Buy paint during holiday sales (Black Friday, Memorial Day)
- Get at least 3 quotes for everything
- Consider "oops paint" for primer/closets
- Shop carpet sales (often end of month)

### ROI Focus

**Spend money on:**

- Good quality paint in main areas (biggest impact)
- Professional photos (critical for online appeal)
- Staging consultation at minimum (expert advice)

**Save money on:**

- High-end carpet (mid-range is fine)
- Fancy door (simple fiberglass is perfect)
- Full staging if budget-tight (do partial)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1.  **Choosing trendy colors** - Stick to neutrals for resale
2.  **Skipping paint samples** - Always test before buying
3.  **Forgetting carpet lead time** - Order early!
4.  **Cheap paint** - Spend on quality paint, it shows
5.  **Over-personalizing** - Remember: you're selling to others
6.  **Skipping professional photos** - Worth every penny
7.  **Not getting enough quotes** - 3 minimum for major work
8.  **Starting without a plan** - Use these docs to stay organized!
