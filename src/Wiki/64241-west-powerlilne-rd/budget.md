## Cost-Saving Opportunities
1. **DIY where possible:**
   - Remove curtains yourself ($0 vs hiring)
   - Paint yourself ($2,000-3,000 savings)
   - Stage with existing furniture (up to $3,000 savings)

2. **Shop smart:**
   - Wait for paint sales (30-40% off holidays)
   - Compare carpet quotes from 3+ vendors
   - Use contractor-grade materials (better prices)

3. **Prioritize high-impact:**
   - Fresh paint gives biggest ROI
   - Clean, neutral carpet in bedrooms crucial
   - Professional photos non-negotiable

4. **Budget alternatives:**
   - Virtual staging instead of physical ($100 vs $1,500+)
   - Clean and paint cabinets instead of replace
   - Partial staging (living room + master only)
