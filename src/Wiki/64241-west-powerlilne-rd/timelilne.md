## Buffer Time & Contingencies

**Built-in buffers:**

- 1 week between painting and carpet (drying, touch-ups)
- 1 week for unexpected delays
- Weather delays for door installation
- Material delivery delays

**If you're behind schedule:**

- Priority 1: Paint quality (most impact)
- Priority 2: Clean carpet in bedrooms
- Priority 3: Professional staging
- Can compress: Cleaning timeline
- Can skip: Virtual tour, drone photos

## Seasonal Considerations

**November/December challenges:**

- Shorter daylight hours for photography
- Holiday delays (Thanksgiving, Christmas)
- Cold weather for exterior work (door installation)
- Contractor availability around holidays

**January listing:**

- Less competition (fewer listings)
- Serious buyers (not just browsing)
- Can take advantage of tax season buyers
- Weather may affect showings

* * *

## Notes

- Timeline assumes professional contractors for all work
- DIY work may take 2-3x longer
- Paint drying time requires good weather/ventilation
- Carpet lead time can be 2-4 weeks - order early!
- Photography should be done on sunny day, 10am-2pm for best light
- Build in extra time for holidays and weather delays
